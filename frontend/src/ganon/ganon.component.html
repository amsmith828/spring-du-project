Ganondorf King of the Gerudos

Ganondorf is the best Smash Ultimate Character, fit only for champions. He not only excels in power and aggression, but is nonpareil in disrespect.

Attacks

 Basic
  • A: neutral attack (11%)
  • A + forward: forward smash (24%)
  • A + up: up smash (21-24%)
  • A + down: down smash (1 hit 5%, 2 hits 15%)
  • A + dash: dash attack (11-15%)
 Tilt
  • A + forward (holding): forward tilt (11%)
  • A + up (holding): Volcano Kick (sourspot 13%, sweetspot 24%)
  • A + down (holding): Leg Sweep (14%)
 Aerial
  • A: nair (1 hit 7%, 2 hits 12%)
  • A + forward: fair (17-18%)
  • A + back: bair (17-18.5%)
  • A + down: dair (17-19%)
  • A + up: up air (6-13%)
 Grab
  • Z: grab (-)
  • A (once grabbed): pummel (1.6%)
  • Forward (once grabbed): forward throw (8%)
  • Back (once grabbed): back throw (5%)
  • Up (once grabbed): up throw (3%)
 Special
  • B: Warlock Punch (30-37%)
  • B + forward: Flame Choke (12-15%)
  • B + down: Wizard's Foot (14-16%)
  • B + up: Dark Dive (11%)
